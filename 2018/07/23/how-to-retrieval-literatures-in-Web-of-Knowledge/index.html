Field tags

Field tag example Explanation
AD=Address AD=Max Planck Finds records in which Max Planck appears in the Address field.
AD=Address AD=Max Planck SAME Mainz Finds records in which Max Planck appears in the same address as Mainz.
AI=Author Identifiers AI=(A-1009-2008 OR 0000-0002-1553-596X) finds records in which either the Researcher ID A-1009-2008 or the ORCID identifier 0000-0002-1553-596X appears in the Author Identifiers table.
AU=Author AU=Zhang Xing Finds records of articles written by this author.
CF=Conference CF=(Component Engineering AND Canada AND 2004) Finds records in which the following conference information appears in the Conference Information field.
CI=City CI=Philadelphia Finds records in which this city appears in the Addresses field.
CU=Country/Region CU=Canada Finds records in which this country appears in the Addresses field.
DO=DOI DO=10.1056/NEJMoa OR DO=10.1016/S0140-6736 Finds records that begin with either DOI (Digital Object Indentifier) number.
ED=Editor ED=Korczyn AD Finds records of articles that were reviewed by this editor.
FG=Grant Number FG=IRG-58-00* Finds records in which this grant number appears in the Grant Number field within the Funding Acknowledgment table. For example:　IRG-58-007-IRG
FO=Funding Agency FO=Academy of Natural Sciences Finds records in which the name of this organization appears in the Funding Agency field within the Funding Acknowledgment table. For example:Academy of Natural Sciences, Philadelphia (ANSP)
FT=Funding Text FT=Shanxi Normal University finds records in which Shanxi Normal University appears in the Funding Text field.
GP=Group Author GP=Magic Collaboration Finds records in which this group author appears in the Group Author(s) field.
IS=ISSN/ISBN IS=1367-4803 Finds records of publications such as journals and electronic publications that contains this ISSN (International Standard Serial Number) number in the ISSN field within a record.
OG=Organization - Enhanced OG=Cornell University Finds all records that contain this preferred organization name and all its name variants, such as:1. Cornell Univ, Ithaca, NY 14853 USA,2. Cornell Univ, Dept Biol & Environm Engn, Ithaca, NY 14853 USA
OG=Organization - Enhanced OG=Cornell Law Sch A search on a name variant returns all records that contain the variant. For example, Cornell Law Sch returns all records that contain Cornell Law Sch in the Addresses field.
OG=Organization - Enhanced OG=(Japan Science “and” Technology Agency (JST)) When searching for organization names that contain a Boolean (AND, NOT, NEAR, and SAME), always enclose the word in quotation marks ( “ “ ).
OO=Organization
PMID=PubMed ID
PS=Province/State PS=NY Finds records in which this state (New York) appears in the Addresses field.
PY=Year Published PY=2007 AND SO=Well Being Finds records of articles that appear in Well Being published in 2007.
SA=Street Address SA=Rockville Pike Finds records in which this street address appears in the Addresses field of a Full Record.
SG=Suborganization SG=Dept BiochemFinds records in which this suborganization appears in the Addresses field of a Full Record.
SO=Publication Name SO=Nature AND TS=Amphibian* Finds records of articles that appear in the journal Nature that contain the term amphibian.
SO=Publication Name SO=Nature AND TS=Amphibian* AND PY=2001 Finds records of articles published in the journal Nature in 2001 that contain the term amphibian.
SO=Publication Name SO=Well Being AND TS=Child* Finds records of articles that appear in the journal called Well Being that contains the Topic term child (or children).
SU=Research Area SU=Computer Science finds records in which this term appears in the Research Areas field of a Full Record. These records may also include other research areas such as:Chemistry, Computer Science
TI=Title TI=succinic acid finds records in which the terms succinic and acid appear in the title.
TI=Title TI=”succinic acid” finds records in which the exact phrase succinic acid appears in the title.
TI=Title TI=(Sanit AND (Fruit OR Vegetable*)) Finds records of articles containing the terms sanitary, sanitize, or sanitizing and either the terms fruit (or fruits) and/or vegetable (or vegetables) in the title of an article.
TS=Topic TS=Monte Carlo simulation finds records of publications containing the terms Monte, Carlo and simulation in any Topic field. The terms do not have to appear together in the same field.
TS=Topic TS=”Monte Carlo simulation” finds records of articles containing the exact phrase Monte Carlo simulation in a Topic field.
TS=Topic TS=(Fish AND Batter AND Chip)Finds records containing the terms fish and batter (or batters and battered) and chip (or chips) in the Abstract, Title, and/or Keywords fields of a record.
UT=Accession Number UT=WOS:A1991FV12500042 Finds the record that is associated with this unique accession number.
UT=Accession Number UT=1991FV125000 Finds all records that contain these characters in the accession number.
WC=Web of Science Category WC=(Archaeology NOT Anthropology) Finds records of publications in which the term Archaelogy appears in the Web of Science Categories field within a record, but all records in which the term Anthropology appears in the Web of Science Categories field are excluded from the search.
ZP=Zip/Postal Code
